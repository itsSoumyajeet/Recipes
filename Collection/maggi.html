Chilli Butter Maggi:

Description-

(Chef SJ's Special...a hostel wala invention)

Ingredients:

  • Maggi
  • Amul wala Butter
  • Multi-purpose Green Chillies
  • Maggi Masaala

Steps to make:

  1. Boil some water.
  2. Put in the maggi. (don't break it, else i'll make sure you won't be able to walk for the next few days..)
  3. Add the masaala, toss in some, chopped chillies.
  4. Strain the extra water(if any).
  5. Add some butter, mix it properly.
  6. Serve.

NOTE:D on't mess up the Maggi:Water ratio, warna tumhe bhagban bhi bachaa sakte.
